Jarrod Parks

Senior Information Systems Major

My name is Jarrod Parks and I am a Senior Computer Information Systems major at Howard University. Throughout my three years at Howard I have been involved in many different organizations, activities, and initiatives. I am most proud of gaining membership into the Executive Leadership Honors program and becoming a proud member of the International Fraternity of Delta Sigma Pi, Iota Rho Chapter.

In 2013, I was elected by my peers to be the President of the School of Business Student Council, where we organized various events both professional and community focused. Because of our hard work we were named the Student Government of the Year and Organization of the Year by Howard Administration. Back home I have two siblings, my twin sister and little brother, and my parents Jonathan and Tachika. Upon graduation I plan to work for a top technology-consulting firm, and eventually earn my MBA from Stanford.
